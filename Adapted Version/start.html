Solum Mysterium

A Soil Mystery



You wake up to a dark room, slowly remembering your life like you do each day. Remember why you exist and all that. For example, you go by they/them.
And you work at an agency with your roommate. Right. So, why did you wake up? Well, said roommate is using really loud unintelligible whispers. You can tell they're whispering, but you can't tell what about. What do you do?
