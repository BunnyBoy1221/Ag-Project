A Soil Mystery



You enter the library and plop down with a book about soil.
It says, “Mineral matter is non-living substances that come from
weathered rock to form soil. Air and water occupy pore spaces
between soil particles. Organic matter is the accumulation of
decayed or partially decayed plants and animals, roots, and living organisms.
Sand is the largest size particle, Silt is the mid-sized soil particle.
Clay is the smallest sized particle.” Well that’s helpful.
