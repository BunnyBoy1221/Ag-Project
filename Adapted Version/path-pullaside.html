A Soil Mystery



You pull aside one of the idle workers to feign conversation.
"What the-" they say in surprise.
"Quiet. I need to figure out what's going on."
You listen for the conversation next to your over the
babble of the workplace.
“So, where do we want to go first?” asked Matt Rodgers.
They just had to choose him.
“I found the appearance of pinworms pretty unusual.”
said your roommate, Charles Stork.
Finally escaping the shock of being suddenly pulled to the side,
the person has a chance to speak.
“What’s this all about? What’s so important that they wouldn’t tell you?”
“I already tried asking, now be quiet, this is important!”
Over the talking of the worker, you couldn’t make out the
rest of the conversation, aside from Clark Bunner and Hannah Roman walking away.
“Now look at what you’ve done.” You say impatiently.
“I can try to help.”
“You’ve done quite enough actually.”
I guess you’ll need to do some more investigating.
